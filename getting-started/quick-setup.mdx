## Connect Your Casino Accounts

After installing the Sweeps extension, you'll need to connect your social casino accounts so Sweeps can collect daily bonuses on your behalf.

<Tip>
  The [Resources page](https://sweeps-app.com/resources) has a **Site Collection Setup video** that walks through this process step by step.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sweeps extension">
    Click the Sweeps icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your casino accounts">
    Connect your social casino accounts through the extension. If you don't have casino accounts yet, check the [Casino Guide](https://sweeps-app.com/casino-guide) to see which sites are supported and their signup bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let Sweeps collect">
    Once your accounts are connected, Sweeps begins collecting daily bonuses automatically in the background. Keep Chrome running and your computer online.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Up Casino Accounts

If you're new to social casinos, you'll need to create accounts on the casino sites before connecting them to Sweeps. The [Casino Guide](https://sweeps-app.com/casino-guide) on the main site lists every supported casino along with:

- **Daily bonus** amounts
- **Signup bonuses** available for new accounts
- Which casinos are currently active on the extension

<Note>
  Most users set up 20–30 casino accounts in their first weekend. Each account takes a few minutes to create on the casino's website.
</Note>

## Tips for Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use a dedicated email account">
    Create a separate Google email account for your social casino signups. This keeps promotional emails from 50+ casinos out of your personal inbox and makes account management much easier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use a dedicated bank account for redemptions">
    When you're ready to redeem earnings, use a dedicated online bank account for added security. Look for FDIC-insured options with no monthly fees.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Start with the highest-value casinos">
    Check the [Casino Guide](https://sweeps-app.com/casino-guide) for casinos with the best daily and signup bonuses. Focus on these first to maximize your early earnings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Play smart when redeeming">

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Keep Sweeps Running

For 24/7 bonus collection:

- **Chrome must be running** — The extension works within Chrome, so keep it open (it can be minimized).
- **Computer must be awake** — Adjust your sleep settings or use a dedicated machine.
- **Stay connected** — A stable internet connection is required.
