## Verifying Collection

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm how users verify that collection is working — dashboard, notifications, logs? */}

After enabling auto-collection, you can verify it's working by:

1. **Checking the extension popup** — Connected casinos will show a green status indicator when bonuses have been successfully collected.
2. **Reviewing the activity log** — The extension tracks each collection attempt and its result.
3. **Logging into the casino directly** — Your bonus balance should reflect the collected daily bonus.

<Note>
  It may take up to 24 hours for the first automated collection to occur, depending on when the casino resets its daily bonuses.
</Note>
