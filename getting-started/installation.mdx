## Requirements

<Info>
  **Before you begin**, make sure you have:
  - Google Chrome browser
  - A computer that can stay online for automated collection
  - Be 18+ and reside in the US or Canada
</Info>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Start by signing up at [sweeps-app.com](https://sweeps-app.com/signin/signup). It takes about 30 seconds and no credit card is required.

## Step 2: Install the Extension

After creating your account, install the Sweeps Chrome extension on your machine.

<Tip>
  The [Resources page](https://sweeps-app.com/resources) has a step-by-step **Installation Guide video** that walks through the entire process.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Set Up a Dedicated Email (Recommended)

Set up a dedicated Google email account for Sweeps and your social casino accounts. This keeps all your casino-related communications, bonuses, and promotional offers organized in one place.

<Warning>
  Using a separate email for your casino accounts helps you stay organized and avoid cluttering your primary inbox with promotional emails from 50+ sites.
</Warning>
