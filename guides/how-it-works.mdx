## Security & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are my casino credentials stored?">
    {/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm exact credential storage method — local encrypted storage, cloud-based, etc. */}
    Your casino login credentials are stored securely using encrypted local storage within the Chrome extension. They are never sent to external servers beyond what's needed to log into the casino sites themselves.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Sweeps access my personal data?">
    No. Sweeps only interacts with the sweepstakes casino sites you explicitly connect. It does not read your browsing history, access other websites, or collect personal data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can Sweeps make purchases or place bets?">
    No. Sweeps is strictly limited to collecting free daily bonuses. It cannot make deposits, place bets, or perform any financial transactions on your behalf.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Running 24/7

For Sweeps to collect bonuses around the clock:

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm the exact requirements for 24/7 operation — does Chrome need to be open? Does the computer need to be awake? Is there a cloud-based option? */}

- **Chrome must be running** — The extension operates within the browser, so Chrome needs to be open (it can be minimized).
- **Computer must be awake** — Your computer's sleep settings should be configured to keep it active, or you can use a dedicated machine.
- **Internet connection required** — A stable internet connection is needed for Sweeps to access casino sites.

<Tip>
  Many users run Sweeps on a dedicated laptop or desktop that stays on 24/7 for uninterrupted collection.
</Tip>

## What Bonuses Does Sweeps Collect?

Sweeps collects **free daily bonuses** offered by sweepstakes casinos. These typically include:

- **Daily login bonuses** — Free coins awarded just for signing in each day
- **Timed bonuses** — Bonuses that reset every few hours
- **Promotional offers** — Free bonus claims from ongoing promotions

<Note>
  Sweeps does not interact with paid features, VIP programs, or any mechanic that requires spending real money. Only free bonuses are collected.
</Note>
