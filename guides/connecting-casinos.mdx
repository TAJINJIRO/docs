## Adding a Casino

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sweeps extension">
    Click the Sweeps icon in your Chrome toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Casino">
    {/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm exact button label and location in the UI */}
    Select the **Add Casino** button from the main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a casino">
    Browse or search the list of 50+ supported casinos. Click on the one you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    {/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm the credential entry flow — direct input, OAuth, browser-based login? */}
    Provide your existing login credentials (email/username and password) for the casino site. If you don't have an account yet, you'll need to create one on the casino's website first.

    <Tip>
      The [Resources page](https://sweeps-app.com/resources) has quick-start guides for creating accounts at the highest-paying casinos.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the connection">
    Sweeps will attempt a test login to verify your credentials. Once verified, the casino appears in your connected list with a green status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must already have an account on the casino site before connecting it to Sweeps. Sweeps does not create casino accounts on your behalf.
</Warning>

## Managing Connections

### Connection Status

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm the exact status indicators and their meanings */}

Each connected casino shows a status indicator:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 🟢 Active | Connected and collecting bonuses normally |
| 🟡 Attention | Requires action (e.g., password change, CAPTCHA) |
| 🔴 Disconnected | Unable to log in — credentials may be outdated |

### Updating Credentials

If a casino shows a yellow or red status, you may need to update your credentials:

1. Click on the casino in your connected list.
2. Select **Update Credentials**.
3. Enter your new login information.
4. Sweeps will re-verify the connection.

### Pausing a Casino

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm if pause functionality exists */}

You can temporarily pause collection for a specific casino without removing it:

1. Click on the casino in your connected list.
2. Toggle **Auto-Collect** off for that casino.
3. Toggle it back on when you're ready to resume.

## Removing a Casino

To disconnect a casino from Sweeps:

1. Click on the casino in your connected list.
2. Select **Remove Casino** (or the trash icon).
