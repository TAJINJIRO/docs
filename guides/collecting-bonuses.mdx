## Automatic Collection

Once you've connected your casinos and enabled auto-collect, Sweeps handles everything. The extension runs in the background and collects bonuses as they become available.

### What Gets Collected

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm the exact bonus types and coin terminology used across different casinos */}

Sweepstakes casinos typically offer two types of free currency:

- **Gold Coins (GC)** — Used for social/free play with no cash value
- **Sweeps Coins (SC)** — Can be redeemed for real prizes or cash equivalents

Sweeps collects both types of bonuses when available.

## Monitoring Your Collections

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm dashboard features — is there a web dashboard, extension-only view, or both? */}

### Extension Dashboard

Open the Sweeps extension to see an overview of your collection activity:

- **Today's collections** — Bonuses collected in the current 24-hour period
- **Total earnings** — Cumulative coins collected across all casinos
- **Per-casino breakdown** — Individual collection status for each connected casino

### Activity Log

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm if an activity log exists and what information it shows */}

The activity log provides a detailed history of every collection attempt:

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Timestamp | When the collection was attempted |
| Casino | Which casino site |
| Status | Success, failed, or skipped |
| Amount | Coins collected (if successful) |
| Notes | Any relevant details (e.g., "Bonus not yet available") |

## Collection Timing

{/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm how collection timing works — fixed schedule, smart timing, user-configurable? */}

Sweeps automatically determines the best time to collect each casino's bonus based on their reset schedule. Most casinos reset daily bonuses at midnight in their local timezone.

<Tip>
  You don't need to configure collection times. Sweeps handles the scheduling automatically to maximize your bonus collection.
</Tip>

## Handling Collection Issues

Sometimes a collection attempt may fail. Common reasons and solutions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="CAPTCHA required">
    {/* PLACEHOLDER: Confirm how Sweeps handles CAPTCHAs */}
    Some casinos present CAPTCHAs during login. When this happens, Sweeps will notify you and pause collection for that casino until you manually solve the CAPTCHA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Casino site is down">
    If a casino's website is temporarily unavailable, Sweeps will retry automatically at the next scheduled interval.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Password changed">
    If you've changed your password on a casino site, update it in Sweeps via the [connection management](/guides/connecting-casinos#updating-credentials) settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bonus already claimed">
    If you manually claimed a bonus before Sweeps could collect it, the extension will skip that collection cycle and try again at the next reset.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Maximizing Your Earnings

- **Connect more casinos** — The more casinos you connect, the more daily bonuses you collect.
- **Keep Sweeps running 24/7** — Ensure your computer stays on and Chrome remains open.
- **Keep credentials current** — Update passwords promptly when you change them on casino sites.
- **Check for new casinos** — Sweeps regularly adds support for new sites. Check the [supported casinos list](/reference/supported-casinos) periodically.

<Note>
  Users who connect 20+ casinos and run Sweeps 24/7 typically earn **$300–$500/month** in redeemable coins. Results vary based on individual casino bonus structures and availability.
</Note>
