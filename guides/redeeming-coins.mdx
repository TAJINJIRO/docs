Common redemption options across sweepstakes casinos include:

- **Gift cards** — Amazon, Visa, and other popular brands
- **Cryptocurrency** — Bitcoin and other supported coins
- **Bank transfer / ACH** — Direct deposit to your bank account
- **Online wallets** — PayPal, Skrill, or similar services

<Note>
  Available redemption methods vary by casino. Check each casino's terms for specific options and minimum amounts.
</Note>
